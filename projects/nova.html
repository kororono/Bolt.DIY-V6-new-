ATLAS

Global Exploration Documentary Series

Client

WorldView Media

Services

Cinematography, Aerial Photography, Post-Production

Year

2024

ATLAS is a six-part documentary series capturing the stories of environmental conservation efforts across diverse global ecosystems. Our team traveled to six continents, documenting the work of dedicated scientists and local communities fighting to preserve endangered habitats.

The series combines breathtaking aerial cinematography, intimate interviews, and pioneering time-lapse techniques to create an immersive viewing experience that connects audiences with these vital conservation stories.
